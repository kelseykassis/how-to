HOW TO PUNCH

NEEDLE

Tools

  • punch needle
  • yarn
  • fabric
  • embroidery hoop
  • scissors
  • marker/pen
  • threader

Punch needle is a relatively easy form of rug making and embroidery that works by creating a looped thread on the back.

  1. Stretch fabric in hoop nice and tight
  2. Draw design on fabric
  3. Thread your punch needle by inserting threader at the needle, and pull yarn from bottom. Then thread through the hole in the needle.
  4. Start Punching! Make sure to keep your stiches even, and always punch until your needle hits the fabric
